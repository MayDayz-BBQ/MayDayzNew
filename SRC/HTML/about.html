FOUNDER
Edward May

Welcome to Maydayz Smokn BBQ - where smoke meets soul. Founded in 2024 by Charlotte native and pitmaster Ed May, Maydayz Smokn BBQ is more than just a BBQ joint - it's a family-run passion project built on bold flavor, slow-smoked tradition, and a whole lotta love. We specialize in authentic smoked BBQ, cooked low and slow to juicy, smoky perfection. From tender brisket to ribs that fall off the bone, every bite is seasoned with our signature house-made rub - a carefully crafted blend of spices that brings out the best in every cut. And lets not forget our sauces - the stars of the show. Our rich cherry glaze and zesty tangy blend are both made in-house, pairing perfectly with everything we smoke. They're sweet, they're bold, and they've got just the right kick. At Maydayz, BBQ is personal. We're a family-managed crew serving up real-deal flavor with Southern charm. Whether you're stopping by for your first plate or your fiftieth, we'll treat you like one of our own: plate stacked high, napkins ready. Maydayz Smokn BBQ - WE SMOKED OUT.
